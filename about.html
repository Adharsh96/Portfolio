ABOUT.

I'm Adharsh M R — someone who fell in love with building things on screens and never really stopped. My journey started with curiosity about how websites work, which led me through a Computer Science degree, freelance design projects, and now an MBA focused on Data Science & Business Analytics. I don't fit neatly into one box, and I've stopped trying to.

01

THE DEVELOPER

I write code that works and reads well. My development approach leans toward simplicity — clear structure, no unnecessary complexity, and layouts that adapt to any screen size. I've worked across front-end stacks and enjoy the process of turning a rough wireframe into a living, breathing interface.

02

THE DESIGNER

Good design, in my view, is when someone uses your work and doesn't notice it's designed at all. I focus on cohesive layouts, thoughtful type pairings, and colour choices that serve the message. My visual work ranges from web interfaces to brand pieces, illustrations, and motion work.

03

THE ANALYST

Numbers tell stories that pixels can't. I'm currently building my toolkit in data analytics, machine learning, and AI — learning to ask better questions and find patterns that matter. For me, data science isn't separate from design; it's another lens to understand the world through.
